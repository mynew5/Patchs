<h1 id="top_info">
Extended Armory adds character talents preview and arena teams to the original armory.
</h1>

<font style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;">Features:</font><br />
- Class colored class name.<br />
- Faction colored guild name.<br />
- Full character Talents preview.<br />
- Full character Glyphs preview.<br />
- Dual spec Talents and Glyphs.<br />
- Talent Tooltips.<br />
- Glyph Tooltips.<br />
- Character Arena Teams preview.<br />
- Main and Secondary Professions.<br />
- Recent Achievements with simple tooltips.<br />
- Extended Item Tooltips (descripton below).<br />
- Item icons are now being resolved locally (Solves missing icons).<br />
- Fixed items for TrinityCore 4.3.4 (Since the cata emu has no items in the DB).<br />
- Fixed Honor and Arena points for cataclysm emulators.<br />

<br />
<font style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;">Extended Item Tooltips Features:</font><br />
- Item Heroic flag.<br />
- Character gems.<br />
- Socket Bonuses.<br />
- Character enchants.<br />
- Character set parts.<br />
- Item Sets.<br />
- Set Bonuses.<br />
- Meta gem requirements.<br />
- Fixed items Armor and Demage for Cataclysm emulators.<br />

<br />
<font style="font-size: 16px; font-weight: bold;">Supported Emulators:</font><br />
- TrinityCore - 3.5.5a<br />
- TrinityCore - 4.3.4<br />
- SkyfireEmu - 4.0.6a<br />
- ArkCORE - 4.0.6a<br />
- Mangos - 3.5.5a<br />
- ArcEmu - 3.5.5a<br />
